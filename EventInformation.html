Workshops

Each semester we run multple workshops in computing. Whether your a studying a computing based degree or not everyone is welcome! Our workshops are great fun and range in activities. From coding to digital design the workshops have something for everyone. Whether your wanting to learn something new and bolster your skills or colloborate with new people and share ideas the workshops are a great place for it all.

RGUHack

RGUHack is an annual event created by students for students. Where theres games, new tech, lots of food and networking oportunties with sponsors. If you don't mind not sleeping part of RGUHack is the Hackathon which is a 24 hour contest in which you work as a team to complete challenges set by sponsors for prizes. RGUHack brings together students across Scotland so if you don't feel the Hackathon is for your it is still a great place to meet new people and make business contacts.

Computing Ball

The ball is our biggest social event of the year! It is also the last event in our calender and in our opinion is the best way to see out the academic year. Celeberate with your class mates and have a good old song and dance. Members of the society get exclusive discounts and early access tickets to this must-go-to event.
